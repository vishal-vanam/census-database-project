Services

User -> He has the right only to login in as a user if he already has an account , otherwise if he is a new user then he has to create a new username and a password and then login. He only has the right to add new records or modify existing records. He can modify only his data.
Admin -> He will be having the data of all the individuals and the data of all the regions.
He can access any query from his data and calculate all the statistical information and give the report on his survey.

This project deals with the survey of different people of varied locations discussing about numerous aspects such as:
      ->Name
      ->Date of Birth
      ->Religion
      ->Age
      ->Sex
      ->Ethnics
      ->Occupation.
and different aspects about a location such as:
      ->Schools
      ->Hospitals
      ->Malls
      ->Offices in that area.
and the Queries may be like:
      ->Crime Rate of the region.
      ->Resources to be focused on for the development of the region.
      ->Emergency services such as near by hospitals etc...
